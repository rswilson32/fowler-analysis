An Overview

The Fowler Museum explores art and artifacts from all around the world. Its collection spans 55 modern-day countries and multiple continents. For further analysis, we will divide the collection into subregions: the Americas, Africa, Asia and Oceania.

In their own words, "The Fowler Museum at UCLA explores global arts and cultures with an emphasis on Africa, Asia, the Pacific, and the Indigenous Americas," and that is clear in this distribution. A majority of piece are from South America, Central Africa and West Africa. We also see a fair amount of pieces are from Asia as well.
